Hi, I’m Eli. A designer who believes every pixel carries intention.

I’ve spent years working at the intersection of strategy and aesthetics — helping brands find their visual voice and building experiences that earn attention. My work spans brand systems, digital products, editorial, and everything in between.

Whether it’s a logo on the back of a business card or a full-scale digital platform, I bring the same obsessive care to every detail.
